Our Retail Legacy

From traditional flavors to aromatic excellence, explore the brands that define Jai Agro Industries Limited.

Premium Spices

SKP Raja Rani

A brand with heritage and evolution.

Originally launched in 2011 as our flagship retail offering, Raja Rani transitioned to wholesale in 2014 to power our B2B expansion. This decade of wholesale mastery has allowed us to perfect our sourcing and processing standards.

As of 2025, we are reintroducing this legacy brand to the retail market. Backed by 15 years of consumer trust and comprehensive marketing support, we are combining heritage with a fresh focus on the modern kitchen.

Traditional Mukhwas

Ras Paan

Born from an exclusive partnership with Jamnagar's legendary Jamnagri mukhavas, a family institution serving authentic flavors since 1980.

Now in its 4th generation of expertise, Ras Paan represents a fusion of traditional craftsmanship and modern distribution. We leverage their authentic Jamnagari recipes and our logistical strength to bring these time-honored mouth fresheners to a national audience.

Aromatic Incense

Parampara

Our premier in-house incense brand, developed alongside production partners with heritage dating back to the 1990s.

Parampara is a testament to our internal design capabilities; from the initial aromatic concept to the final premium packaging, every detail is crafted by the Jai Agro team. It reflects our unwavering commitment to quality as we expand into new lifestyle categories.
